Canadian Sphynx

Sphynxes are known for their extroverted behavior.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Size: 6-12 lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 8-14 years
  • Habitat: The human heart

The largely hairless Sphynx is very people-oriented and affectionate. They are highly intelligent, playful, cuddly, often described as being "part dog, part child, part monkey, and part cat". They display a high level of energy, intelligence, curiosity, and affection for their owners.
